About us

Project details

What the service does

Invoice Generator helps users create professional invoices without starting from a blank template. It supports labor, materials, fees, tax, discounts, print preview, and saved PDF copies.

Who it helps

Contractors, freelancers, mobile repair workers, cleaners, tutors, landscapers, and small businesses that need quick, customer-ready invoices.

Common questions

What is this?
Invoice Generator is a free tool for creating professional invoices without starting from a blank page. Add business and customer details, list what you're billing for, and get a print- or email-ready invoice.
Who built it?
Brian Phillips, as a CSC 670 course project.
Why?
Contractors and freelancers often don't have a fast, no-fuss way to put together a clean invoice on the spot. This project is focused on getting that one thing right.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. Professional and Business become available to switch into as soon as they launch.
Is Basic really free?
Yes. Creating, printing, and saving invoices costs nothing on the Basic plan.
What happens to my invoices?
They stay yours. Nothing is watermarked or locked behind a future paywall.
When will the future updates happen?
There's no fixed date yet. Saved history, customers, and export tools are planned as the project continues past this course module.
Is this maintained and kept up to date?
It's actively being built as part of an ongoing course project. The Basic plan is the stable, working version; anything marked Planned or Future isn't live yet.
Is my information safe?
Everything you type, including your logo and watermark image, stays in your browser on your own device. Nothing is uploaded to a server.
